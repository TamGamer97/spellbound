How to play

Find words just like in Spelling Bee. Four letters minimum, and you must use the center letter. Scoring: 4 letters = 4 pts, 5 letters = 6 pts, 6 = 8, 7 = 10, 8 = 12 (longer words score more). Pangrams get an extra +5 bonus.

Words found by your opponent are taken off the board for you, and vice versa. This applies to all words except pangrams, which are available for both players.

To win, you must score more points in five minutes than your opponent, and have at least one pangram.

Once time is up you can keep looking for words, but the game is over.

Pro tip: use a keyboard for maximum speed.
